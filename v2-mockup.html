Common Questions

What's the capacity? +
We host intimate gatherings for up to 25 seated guests. Larger private events available on request.
How do I secure my booking? +
Choose your booking type and reserve your date. We'll invoice you within 48 hours. Once your 50% reservation fee is received, your booking is confirmed. If we don't receive payment within 48 hours, the booking will be released.
Can I bring my own food & drinks? +
Absolutely. Our kitchen includes a fridge, 5-hob stove, microwave, and double oven. Outside catering is welcome. We don't provide cooking utensils, and the space must be left as you found it.
What's the cancellation policy? +
30+ days: full refund or free reschedule. 15-30 days: 50% refund or one free reschedule. 7-14 days: non-refundable, £25 admin fee for reschedule. Less than 7 days: forfeited. No-show: full amount charged.
Is parking available? +
Paid parking on nearby streets. Free after 6:30 PM and all day Sundays. 30-minute loading zone at the entrance for drop-offs.
What about setup and pack down? +
Please allow enough time for setup and pack down within your booked slot. All activities including cleanup must be completed within your booked time.
Can I bring a DJ or play music? +
Yes, light music is welcome. DJs welcome for background-style sets, but sound must stay moderate and finish by 10 PM. We don't supply speakers or PA — your DJ must provide their own.
Do you have a full kitchen I can use for filming? +
Yes — 5-hob stove, double oven, fridge, and microwave. Perfect for recipe content, baking videos, and food photography. At £45/hr (content rate), it's a fraction of the cost of dedicated cooking venue hire in London.
