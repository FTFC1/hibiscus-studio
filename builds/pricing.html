Frequently Asked Questions

How do I secure my date?
Book through our website and we'll send an invoice within 48 hours. Pay a 50% reservation fee to confirm your date. Your booking is secured once payment is received.
Can I bring my own food & drinks?
Yes! Our kitchen is available for your use, and outside catering is welcome. We do not provide cooking utensils. No deep fat fryers. Please leave the space as you found it.
What if I run over my booking time?
You will be charged £100 for every hour (or part thereof) that you exceed your booked time.
What about setup and pack-down time?
We include 1 complimentary hour at the end of your booking for pack-down. Please ensure your booked slot includes time for setup.
What equipment is included?
Your hire includes 40 limewash Chiavari chairs and 10 trestle tables (each 6ft x 2ft).
Can I bring a DJ or play music?
Yes, DJs are welcome! Please note: we do not supply speakers or a PA system - these must be brought with you. Music until 10pm.
Is parking available?
Parking is the responsibility of the client. You can park directly outside for up to 30 minutes for loading/unloading (PCNs issued after 30 mins). Free parking on Huntingdon Street after 6:30pm and all day Sunday. Nearby paid options: Morrisons Car Park (free 2hrs), DesParking (99 Rathbone Street), Canning Town Carpark.
