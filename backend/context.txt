Hibiscus Studio – Chatbot Master Knowledge Base (V5)

1. Venue Identity & Pivot
Q: What type of venue is Hibiscus Studio?
A: We are a curated event space specializing in baking & cooking workshops, intimate dinner parties, and creative sessions. We are also the home of bento cake classes by Flour Girls Cakehouse.

Q: Do you host birthday parties?
A: We do host intimate birthday dinners and private dining experiences. However, we have transitioned away from high-energy parties/raves. Our focus is on seated, intimate celebrations with a curated vibe.

2. Capacity & Event Limits
Q: What is the maximum capacity?
A: It depends on the event type:
- Intimate Social Events (Dinner parties, birthday dinners, bridal showers, proposals): Max 25 people (Seated & Standing).
- Workshops & Cooking Classes: Max 40 people (Allowed between 8am–6pm or 6pm–10pm).

Q: Can I have more than 25 people for a dinner party?
A: No, for social gatherings and dinners, our strict limit is 25 guests to maintain the intimate, curated nature of the space.

3. Event Types & Use Cases
Q: What kind of events can I book?
A: We are perfect for:
- Private Dining: Intimate birthday dinners, anniversary dinners, and supper clubs.
- Workshops: Cooking classes, baking sessions (bento cakes), wellness events, and panel talks.
- Life Moments: Proposals, Nikkahs, intimate bridal showers.
- Content: Curated brand shoots and filming.

Q: Who runs the cake classes?
A: We host bento cake classes facilitated by our partner, Flour Girls Cakehouse.

4. Pricing & Upsell Logic
Q: What are the hire rates?
A:
- Full Day (12 hours): £885 (Best Value).
- Half Day (6 hours): £465.

Q: Is the kitchen fully equipped for cooking classes or dinners?
A: Yes. The studio features a kitchenette with a fridge, 5-hob stove, microwave, and double oven, making it ideal for cooking workshops and private dining. (Note: You must provide your own utensils, cookware, and serving equipment unless booked via a specific package.)

Q: How many chairs and tables do you have?
