# Mobile-First LMS Tech Stack Comparison for Nigerian Retail Training Platform

Nigeria's mobile-first retail sales training market demands robust offline capabilities, minimal data usage, and cost-effective scaling. After comprehensive analysis of four distinct tech approaches, the **Telegram bot + PWA + Google Sheets stack emerges as the optimal solution**, offering exceptional value at $25-60/month with superior offline functionality and rapid deployment capabilities specifically designed for Android-dominant, low-connectivity environments.

## Executive Summary

**Top Recommendation: Telegram Bot + Static HTML/PWA + Google Sheets**
- **Cost**: $25-60/month (well within budget)
- **Setup Time**: 4-6 weeks to MVP
- **Mobile Optimization**: Excellent for Nigerian Android users
- **Offline Capabilities**: 80-90% data reduction, full offline learning
- **Maintenance**: Perfect for non-technical founders

**Alternative Recommendation: TalentLMS (Low-Code Solution)**
- **Cost**: $69/month for 40 users

## Risk Mitigation Strategies

### Connectivity Challenges
**Solution**: Aggressive offline caching
- Cache entire course modules during good connectivity
- Progressive sync ensures no data loss
- Offline-first design principles throughout platform

### Data Cost Concerns
**Solution**: Bandwidth optimization
- Compressed media formats (WebP images, optimized videos)
- Progressive loading strategies
- User control over data usage settings

### Technical Complexity
**Solution**: Managed services approach
- Use hosted solutions (Railway, Netlify) for infrastructure
- Google Sheets for non-technical content management
- Comprehensive documentation and training materials

### Scalability Planning
**Solution**: Phased architecture evolution
- Start with Google Sheets for data management
- Migrate to PostgreSQL database at 2,000+ users
- Implement CDN for video content at scale
- Monitor performance metrics for proactive optimization
