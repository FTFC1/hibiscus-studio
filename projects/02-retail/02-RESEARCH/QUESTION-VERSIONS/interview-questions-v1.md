# Interview Questions - Version 1
**Target Audience:** Retail Sales Staff, HR/Owners, Training Managers  
**Focus:** Basic training needs assessment

## Sales Staff Questions

### Current Sales Techniques
1. What sales techniques does your team use most often? (upselling, cross-selling, suggestive selling)
2. From 1-10, how confident are your colleagues when discussing prices with customers?
3. Tell us about a time when a customer was handled very well - what worked and why?

### Customer Conversion
4. How much of your team struggles with turning browsers into buyers?
5. What percentage would you estimate are strong closers?

### Knowledge Gaps
6. Looking at these areas, which do colleagues struggle with most?
   - Product features
   - Pricing/promotions  
   - Return policies
   - Store availability
   - Care instructions

### Customer Objections
7. What are the three most challenging customer objections?
   - "This is too expensive"
   - "I'll think about it"
   - "I'll come back later"
   - "I saw this cheaper online"
   - Product availability issues

### Work Interruptions
8. What takes you away from helping customers?
   - Inventory checks
   - Paperwork/admin
   - Personal calls
   - Training sessions
   - Cleaning/maintenance

### Training Preferences
9. Rank these training methods from most to least effective:
   - Short mobile lessons (10-min videos)
   - In-person workshops
   - Written materials
   - Voice coaching/WhatsApp
   - Watching experienced staff

10. How long could you realistically watch training videos daily?

## HR/Owner Questions

### Current Training Approach
1. How do you currently train sales staff?
2. What's your biggest training challenge?
3. How do you measure training effectiveness?

### Skill Gaps
4. What skills do new hires need most?
5. Where do experienced staff struggle?
6. What customer complaints relate to staff skills?

### Training Constraints
7. What limits your training efforts?
   - Time
   - Budget  
   - Staff availability
   - Resources

### Success Metrics
8. How do you measure sales performance?
9. What would improved training impact most?

## Manager Questions

### Training Delivery
1. How often do you conduct training sessions?
2. What format works best for your team?
3. How do you handle skill gaps quickly?

### Operational Impact
4. How does training time affect operations?
5. What training would have immediate impact?
6. How do you track training effectiveness?
