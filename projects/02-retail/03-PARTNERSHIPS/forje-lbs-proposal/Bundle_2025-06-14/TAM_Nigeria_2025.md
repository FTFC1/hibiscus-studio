# Nigerian Fashion Retail Head-Office Roles: Comprehensive Market Sizing Analysis (2025-2028)

## Executive Summary

Nigeria's fashion retail head-office talent market represents a high-growth opportunity with 2,800-4,200 professional roles across conservative to aggressive scenarios. The sector benefits from strong economic fundamentals (27.5 % workforce in retail trade, $1.19 B fashion market growing at 7-9 % CAGR) but faces critical skills gaps. Key findings: Lagos dominates with 70 % of opportunities, salary ranges from ₦1.2 M-₦7 M annually, and seven critical pain points require intervention. Academic partnerships with institutions like Lagos Business School present viable pathways for addressing workforce-development needs through specialised programmes targeting 150-300 annual cohorts at ₦350 K-₦750 K per participant.

## Methodology Overview

### Analytical Framework
1. **Top-down** – Fashion-market value × staffing ratios.  
2. **Bottom-up** – Aggregated job postings and org-chart modelling.  
3. **Benchmarking** – Adjusted BLS / ONS ratios to Nigerian context.

### Key Data & Triangulation
• NBS Q4-2024 labour force (27.5 % retail).  
• Euromonitor & Statista fashion values.  
• BLS / ONS 1:6–1:8 head-office ratio → adjusted 1:10–1:15.  
• LinkedIn, Jobberman scraping (2 640 live ads May-2025).  
• Salary data: PayScale, Mercer, Robert Walters.
