## ðŸ”¹ DETAILED RESEARCH INSIGHTS

### Sales Staff Interview Part 1
**Key Findings:**
- **Cross-selling** most common technique (ranked #1)
- **Pricing confidence varies** by customer assessment
- **Time constraints** major issue - fast-paced luxury environment
- **Knowledge gaps**: Care instructions (#1), Product features improving
- **Top objections**: "I'll come back later", "I'll think about it", Product availability
- **Training preference**: 10-minute mobile videos > Workshops > WhatsApp
- **Success metric**: Daily sales targets most relevant

**Critical Quote**: "Look at the type of client we're attending to" - customer segmentation approach

### Sales Staff Interview Part 2  
**Key Findings:**
- **Value-selling approach**: Benefits before price, future utility emphasis
- **Luxury positioning**: "It's a luxury store. We step into the store, we feel ready"
- **Follow-up strategy**: Capture details, follow up 2-3 days, create urgency
- **Operational challenges**: Debt submission, corporate calls interrupt customer time
- **Device preference**: Personal phones for training content
- **Data costs concern** - primary barrier to mobile training

**Critical Quote**: "The price will not be an issue since you know the future" - value justification

### Training Constraints & Preferences
**Mobile Training Barriers**:
1. Data costs (primary concern)
2. Internet connectivity 
3. Work interruptions (stock checks, paperwork)

**Preferred Learning Format**:
1. **Visual > Audio > Text** (strong preference)
2. **Personal devices** over company devices
3. **Observation-based** learning most effective
4. **Interactive elements** increase retention

**Optimal Session Length**: 5-10 minutes maximum
