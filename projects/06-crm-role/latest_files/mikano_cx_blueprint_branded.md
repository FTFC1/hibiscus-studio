## üîπ CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS & RISK MITIGATION

### **‚ö†Ô∏è Risk 1: Poor User Adoption**

**The Problem**: Staff see this as extra work

**WIIFM (What's In It For Me)**:
- **Sales**: Prove your work, secure commission
- **Service**: Look professional, have full history
- **Management**: Clear pipeline without chasing

**Mitigation**: Weekly team review with iron rule:
> *"If it's not in the Hub, we don't discuss it"*

**30-Minute Standard Meeting Format**:
1. Pipeline Review (15 mins)
2. Performance Check (5 mins)  
3. Problem Solving (10 mins)

### **‚ö†Ô∏è Risk 2: Data Quality Issues**

**The Problem**: Bad data = useless reports

**3 Simple Rules**:
1. **Search First** - Check before creating
2. **Standard Naming** - Use official company names
3. **Data Stewards** - One person owns quality per division

**Data Steward Role**:
- **1-2 hours/week** reviewing entries
- **Detail-oriented** person from within business
- **Added responsibility**, not new job title

### **‚ö†Ô∏è Risk 3: Misaligned Incentives**

**The Problem**: No reward for teamwork = silos continue

**Mitigation**: Define cross-selling compensation **before** go-live

### **‚ö†Ô∏è Risk 4: Scalability Limits**

**The Problem**: Excel will eventually break

**CRM Review Triggers** (Any 2 = Start Review):
- File >100MB
- File takes >10 seconds to open  
- Data Stewards spend >2 hours/week cleaning
