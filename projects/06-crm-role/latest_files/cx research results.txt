## ðŸ”¹ Response Standards

**12-Hour Rule Implementation**:

**Hour 0-2**: Auto-response acknowledging inquiry
- WhatsApp: "Thank you for your interest! Our sales specialist will contact you within 12 hours"
- Include: Basic info link, showroom locations

**Hour 2-8**: Qualified response
- Sales reviews inquiry, sends personalized message
- Books appointment if customer ready
- Provides specific information requested

**Hour 8-12**: Follow-up if no response
- "Hi [Name], just following up on your [product] inquiry. When would be good to discuss?"

**Escalation**: Manager alerted at hour 10 if no response sent

**Why 12 hours?**: Nigerians expect quick response but understand business hours. 24 hours loses 60% of hot leads.
