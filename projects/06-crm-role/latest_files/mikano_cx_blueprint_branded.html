Account Ownership Framework
Clear, fair rules to prevent ownership disputes while rewarding both relationship building and deal closing.
The Real Problem

Sequential ownership conflicts destroy relationships:

2023: Person A talks to Client → Nothing happens

2024: Person B talks to same Client → Gets fresh lead, closes deal

The Fight: "Whose account is this?"

Traditional solutions create perverse incentives: winner-takes-all encourages stealing, time limits punish annual buyers.

Recommended Solution: Shared Commission

Relationship Builder

30%

Rewards relationship investment

Deal Closer

70%

Rewards actual sales work

Time Decay: After 18 months of no activity, relationship builder's share drops to 0%

Why This Works
  • Rewards both relationship building AND closing
  • Eliminates "stealing" incentives
  • Prevents account hoarding
  • Fair to all parties
Implementation Rules
  • Trigger: Any deal >₦500k gets split commission
  • Evidence: Prior logged interactions in Excel CRM Spreadsheet
  • Authority: Division Head makes final call on disputes

Critical Success Factors & Risk Mitigation
Proactive strategies to prevent the most common failure modes in customer experience initiatives.
Risk 1: Poor User Adoption

The Challenge: Staff see this as extra work

WIIFM (What's In It For Me)

  • Sales: Prove your work, secure commission
  • Service: Look professional, have full history
  • Management: Clear pipeline without chasing

Weekly Team Review - Iron Rule:

"If it's not in the Hub, we don't discuss it"

30-Minute Standard Meeting Format:
  1. Pipeline Review (15 mins)
  2. Performance Check (5 mins)
  3. Problem Solving (10 mins)
Risk 2: Data Quality Issues

The Challenge: Bad data = useless reports

3 Simple Rules
  1. Search First - Check before creating
  2. Standard Naming - Use official company names
  3. Data Stewards - One person owns quality per division
Risk 3: Misaligned Incentives

The Challenge: No reward for teamwork = silos continue

Mitigation: Define cross-selling compensation before go-live

Risk 4: Scalability Limits

The Challenge: Excel will eventually break

CRM Review Triggers (Any 2 = Start Review):
  • File >100MB
  • File takes >10 seconds to open
  • Data Stewards spend >2 hours/week cleaning
