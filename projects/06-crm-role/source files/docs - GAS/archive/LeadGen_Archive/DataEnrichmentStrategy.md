# Data Enrichment Strategy for IPP Prospects

## Corporate Registry Resources
- **Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC)**: [CAC Portal](https://services.cac.gov.ng) - Search for registered businesses
- **Nigerian Stock Exchange**: [NGX Group](https://ngxgroup.com/exchange/companies-profile) - For public companies
- **NIPC Database**: [Nigerian Investment Promotion Commission](https://nipc.gov.ng/viewcompanies.php) - Lists foreign investment companies

## Industry-Specific Sources
- **Manufacturers Association of Nigeria**: [MAN Directory](https://manufacturersnigeria.org/members-directory) - Lists manufacturing companies
- **Nigerian Association of Chambers of Commerce (NACCIMA)**: [Member Directory](https://www.naccima.com/members) - Business network listings
- **Lagos Chamber of Commerce and Industry**: [LCCI Members](https://lagoschamber.com/members) - Lagos-based businesses

## Energy Consumption Data
- **Nigerian Electricity Regulatory Commission**: [NERC](https://nerc.gov.ng) - Regulatory data and reports
- **Ministry of Power**: [Power Sector Reports](https://www.power.gov.ng) - National energy statistics
- **Distribution Companies (DisCos)**: Regional power distribution companies have industrial client listings

## Commercial Databases
- **Kompass Nigeria**: [Business Directory](https://ng.kompass.com) - Paid business database
- **Bloomberg Terminal**: Access to Nigerian corporate data
- **Dun & Bradstreet**: [D&B Nigeria](https://www.dnb.com/business-directory/company-profiles.nigeria.html) - Company profiles

## Four-Step Enrichment Process

1. **Initial Broad List** - Compile from industry associations (25,000+ companies)
2. **Basic Filtering** - Apply size/location filters (5,000+ prospects)
3. **Deep Research** - Conduct detailed research on energy needs (1,000+ qualified leads)
4. **Final Qualification** - Apply Mikano-specific criteria (250+ sales-ready opportunities)
