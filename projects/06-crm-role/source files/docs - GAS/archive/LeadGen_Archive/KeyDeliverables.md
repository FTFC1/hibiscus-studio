# Mikano IPP Project: Key Deliverables

## Core Deliverables

1. **Active Lead Generation System**
   - News monitoring for IPP opportunities
   - Industry event tracking
   - Regulatory announcement analysis
   - Competitor movement alerts

2. **Lead Management Database**
   - Google Sheet with three tabs:
     - **Leads Tab**: Company profiles with unique IDs
     - **Activity Tab**: All interactions linked to companies
     - **Pipeline Tab**: Summary metrics and conversion tracking
   - Automatic lead scoring based on recency and activity

3. **Personalized Outreach System**
   - Email templates customized by industry and news trigger
   - Follow-up sequences based on interaction history
   - Meeting preparation documents

4. **ROI Calculator**
   - Industry-specific savings estimator
   - Comparison with current power solutions
   - Implementation timeline calculator

## News-Driven Lead Generation

### Weekly Lead Generation Process

1. **Monday: News Scanning**
   - Review Nigerian business publications for expansion announcements
   - Monitor regulatory news around power sector
   - Track new investments in manufacturing, hospitality, and commercial sectors
   - Source: BusinessDay, The Guardian, Punch, ThisDay

2. **Tuesday: Lead Qualification**
   - Research companies mentioned in news
   - Estimate power requirements based on facility size/industry averages
   - Identify decision-makers through LinkedIn and company websites
   - Prioritize based on gas availability in their location

3. **Wednesday: Personalized Outreach**
   - Craft personalized emails referencing current news
   - Connect outreach to specific pain points implied by news
   - Sequence follow-ups referencing original news trigger

4. **Thursday: Meeting Preparation**
   - Prepare briefing documents for scheduled meetings
   - Customize ROI calculations for specific prospects
   - Develop objection responses based on industry and news context

5. **Friday: Analysis & Improvement**
   - Review response rates from news-triggered outreach
   - Compare effectiveness of different news categories
   - Adjust approach based on performance data

### News Categories to Monitor

1. **Expansion Announcements**
   - New facilities being built
   - Production capacity increases
   - Entry into new markets requiring facilities

2. **Energy Disruption News**
   - Grid instability in specific regions
   - Diesel price increases
   - Power-related production stoppages

3. **Investment/Funding News**
   - Companies receiving new investment
   - Private equity acquisitions
   - Public offerings indicating growth

4. **Regulatory Developments**
   - Changes to power generation regulations
   - Environmental compliance requirements
   - Industrial zone developments
   - Gas infrastructure announcements

## Implementation Timeline

1. **Week 1: Foundation**
   - Set up Google Sheet with all required tabs
   - Import existing leads from Gas Leads.xlsx
   - Create news monitoring system (using news websites and Google Alerts)

2. **Week 2: Process Implementation**
   - Begin daily news monitoring routine
   - Develop first batch of news-triggered outreach emails
   - Test ROI calculator with initial prospects

3. **Week 3-4: Refinement**
   - Analyze which news sources/categories produce best leads
   - Refine email templates based on response rates
   - Optimize lead scoring based on early results

## Key Performance Indicators

1. **Lead Generation Metrics**
   - Number of news-triggered leads generated weekly
   - Quality score of news-triggered vs. static list leads
   - Conversion rate from news mention to first contact

2. **Pipeline Metrics**
   - Progression of leads through sales stages
   - Average deal size by news trigger category
   - Time from news mention to proposal/close

3. **Efficiency Metrics**
   - Time spent per qualified lead
   - Response rate to news-triggered outreach
   - Number of touchpoints required for meetings

## Technology Implementation

1. **Google Alerts** - For monitoring specific companies and keywords
2. **Google Sheet** - For lead and activity tracking
3. **Email Templates** - Stored in shared Google Drive for easy customization
4. **ROI Calculator** - Simple spreadsheet with industry benchmarks
5. **News Aggregator** - RSS feed of key Nigerian business publications
