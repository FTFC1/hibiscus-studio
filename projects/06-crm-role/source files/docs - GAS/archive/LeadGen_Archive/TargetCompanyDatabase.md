## Prioritization Matrix

### Tier 1 Prospects (High Priority)

1. Nigerian Breweries (Lagos) - High consumption, gas available, multiple locations
2. Dangote Flour Mills - Large conglomerate with multiple facilities
3. Rack Centre - Critical power needs, existing relationship with similar client
4. Eko Hotels - High visibility, showcase potential
5. The Palms Mall - Strategic location, high visibility
6. Nestlé Nigeria - Multinational with sustainability goals
7. Lafarge Africa - High consumption, sustainability initiatives
8. Guinness Nigeria - Multinational with green initiatives
9. MDXi - Critical power needs, tech sector growth
10. Cadbury Nigeria - Multinational with reliability requirements

### Tier 2 Prospects (Medium Priority)

1. PZ Cussons - Good consumption profile, existing infrastructure
2. Nigerian Bottling Co. - Consistent power needs
3. Radisson Blu - International brand standards
4. Flour Mills Nigeria - Large operation, suitable location
5. Unilever Nigeria - Sustainability commitments
6. Sheraton Lagos - International hotel chain
7. Ikeja City Mall - Strategic location
8. Olam Nigeria - Growing operations
9. Friesland Campina - Food production requirements
10. Beta Glass - Energy intensive operations

### Tier 3 Prospects (Longer Term)

1. United Nigerian Textiles - Location challenges, gas access limited
2. Transcorp Hilton - Location challenges, gas access limited
3. Galaxy Backbone - Government affiliated, longer sales cycle
4. African Steel Mills - Gas access limitations
5. Jabi Lake Mall - Location challenges
6. Nichemtex Industries - Sector challenges
7. Nizamiye Hospital - Location challenges
8. Layer3 - Smaller operation, location challenges
9. Neimeth International - Gas access limitations
10. Ibom Hotel & Golf - Remote location, infrastructure challenges

## Contact Status Tracking

- **Initial Research** - Basic information gathered
- **Contact Identified** - Decision maker identified
- **Initial Outreach** - First contact made
- **Engaged** - Responding to communications
- **Meeting Scheduled** - Site visit or call arranged
- **Proposal Stage** - Proposal in development/submitted
- **Negotiation** - Actively discussing terms
- **Closed Won/Lost** - Final outcome determined
