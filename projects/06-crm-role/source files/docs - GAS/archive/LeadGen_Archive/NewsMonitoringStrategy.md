# Nigerian News Monitoring Strategy for IPP Leads

## Core Strategy

Instead of relying on static lead lists, we'll actively monitor Nigerian news sources to identify companies that might benefit from Mikano's IPP solutions based on current events and announcements.

## Key Nigerian News Sources

### Business Publications

1. **BusinessDay Nigeria**
   - URL: [https://businessday.ng/](https://businessday.ng/)
   - Focus: Business news, company announcements, energy sector
   - Sections to monitor: Energy, Manufacturing, Real Estate, Companies

2. **The Guardian Nigeria (Business)**
   - URL: [https://guardian.ng/category/business-services/](https://guardian.ng/category/business-services/)
   - Focus: Business news, infrastructure developments
   - Sections to monitor: Energy, Business News, Industry

3. **ThisDay**
   - URL: [https://www.thisdaylive.com/](https://www.thisdaylive.com/)
   - Focus: General and business news
   - Sections to monitor: Business, Energy

4. **Punch Nigeria (Business)**
   - URL: [https://punchng.com/topics/business/](https://punchng.com/topics/business/)
   - Focus: Business news, company updates
   - Sections to monitor: Business, Energy

### Industry-Specific Sources

5. **Nigeria Electricity Hub**
   - URL: [https://www.nigeriaelectricityhub.com/](https://www.nigeriaelectricityhub.com/)
   - Focus: Power sector news, regulatory updates
   - Why valuable: Regulatory changes that might create IPP opportunities

6. **Nigerian Investment Promotion Commission**
   - URL: [https://nipc.gov.ng/](https://nipc.gov.ng/)
   - Focus: Investment announcements, new projects
   - Why valuable: New facility investments often require power solutions

7. **Manufacturing Association of Nigeria**
   - URL: [https://man.org.ng/](https://man.org.ng/)
   - Focus: Manufacturing sector news, new entrants
   - Why valuable: Direct access to manufacturing companies with high power needs

## News Monitoring Process

### Daily Monitoring (20-30 minutes)

1. **Morning Scan (15 minutes)**
   - Check business sections of major publications
   - Scan headlines for relevant keywords:
     - Expansion, investment, new facility
     - Power challenges, energy costs
     - Manufacturing growth
     - Industrial zone

2. **Keyword Monitoring**
   - Set up Google Alerts for:
     - "Nigeria + new factory"
     - "Nigeria + expansion plan"
     - "Nigeria + power challenges"
     - "Nigeria + industrial development"
     - "Nigeria + manufacturing investment"
     - "Nigeria + diesel costs"

3. **Recording Process**
   - For each relevant article:
     - Save to News Triggers tab in Google Sheet
     - Tag with appropriate category
     - Extract mentioned companies
     - Assign relevance score (1-5)

**Example headline:** "Rising Diesel Costs Force Nigerian Manufacturers to Cut Production"

**Action:** Identify mentioned companies, prepare ROI comparison vs. diesel generation

### 3. Investment & Funding

**Keywords:** secured funding, investment round, private equity, expansion capital, IPO, acquisition

**Action:** Research expansion plans, estimate new power requirements

### 4. Regulatory Developments

**Keywords:** NERC, gas supply, pipeline expansion, environmental regulations, industrial policy

**Example headline:** "New Gas Pipeline to Connect Lagos Industrial Zones"

**Action:** Identify companies in newly connected areas, prepare gas-based IPP proposals

## Converting News to Leads

### Step 1: Initial Assessment (Same Day)

- Determine if mentioned companies match target profile
- Estimate potential power requirements based on industry and announcement
- Check if company already exists in database

### Step 2: Research (Within 24 Hours)

- Find company website and LinkedIn
- Identify decision-makers (operations, facilities, finance)
- Research current power situation if mentioned
- Estimate ROI for IPP solution

### Step 3: Personalized Outreach (Within 48 Hours)

- Craft email specifically referencing the news item
- Connect announcement directly to power requirements
- Offer relevant case study (e.g., similar company in same industry)

### Step 4: Follow-Up (3-5 Days Later)

- Reference original news again
- Add new information if available
- Offer specific next steps (call, meeting, site visit)

## Measuring Effectiveness

### Key Metrics

1. **News-to-Lead Conversion Rate**
   - Number of leads generated per relevant news item
   - Target: 2 leads per relevant news item

2. **News Lead Quality**
   - Percentage of news-generated leads progressing to meeting
   - Target: 30%+ progression to meeting

3. **Response Rate**
   - Percentage of news-triggered emails receiving response
   - Target: 20%+ response rate

### Weekly Analysis

Review every Friday:
- Which news sources produced valuable leads
- Which categories generated highest response rates
- Which templates performed best

## Practical Tips

1. **Timing Matters**
   - Reach out within 48 hours of news publication
   - Reference news as "recent" rather than giving exact date
   - Follow up quickly for news-triggered responses

2. **Relevance Is Key**
   - Connect news directly to power needs
   - Explain why the news makes IPP particularly relevant now
   - Offer insights specific to their announcement

3. **Beyond the News**
   - Use news as entry point, not entire conversation
   - Bring unique insights about implications of their announcement
   - Position as helping them succeed with their announced plans
