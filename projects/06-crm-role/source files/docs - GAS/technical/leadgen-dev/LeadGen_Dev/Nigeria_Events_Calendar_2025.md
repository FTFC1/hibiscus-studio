## üìã **Event Strategy**

### **Pre-Event Preparation**
- Research exhibitor lists 2 weeks before
- Identify target companies by booth location
- Prepare business cards with "Business Developer" title
- Create one-page company capability statement

### **At-Event Tactics**
- Ask: "What's your current power source?"
- Identify: Existing power challenges
- Qualify: Power requirements (MW capacity)
- Connect: Exchange contacts for follow-up

### **Post-Event Follow-up**
- Send connection requests within 48 hours
- Reference specific conversation points
- Offer site visit for power assessment
- Add to CRM with event source tag
