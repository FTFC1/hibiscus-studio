# Critical Questions for Gas Sales Partner Collaboration

## Essential Information Needed from Your Gas Sales Contact

1. **Deal Economics and Conversion**
   - What's your current deal conversion rate from initial contact to signed contract?
   - What's the typical sales cycle length for IPP projects from first meeting to implementation?
   - Which pricing models have proven most effective with Nigerian clients?

2. **Target Market Insights**
   - Which industries have been most receptive to IPP solutions in Nigeria?
   - What company size (by revenue/employee count) represents your sweet spot?
   - Are there specific regions within Nigeria where you've had higher success rates?

3. **Technical Requirements**
   - What are the minimum technical specifications a prospect needs to qualify?
   - Do you have specific gas supply requirements or limitations?
   - What site preparation issues most commonly disqualify otherwise promising leads?

4. **Objection Handling**
   - What are the top 3 objections you encounter when selling IPP solutions?
   - Which decision-makers typically raise the most significant concerns?
   - What competing solutions (beyond diesel generators) do you encounter?

5. **Partnership Structure**
   - What support do you need from Mikano beyond qualified leads?
   - How do you structure commission/revenue sharing on successful deals?
   - What information do you need from us before engaging with a prospect?

## Follow-up Areas Based on Responses

- **For low conversion rates**: What specific qualification improvements would help?
- **For long sales cycles**: Where are the current bottlenecks in your process?
- **For regional variations**: How should our outreach messaging differ by region?
- **For technical barriers**: Can Mikano provide technical assessment support?
- **For pricing objections**: Would flexible financing options increase close rates?
