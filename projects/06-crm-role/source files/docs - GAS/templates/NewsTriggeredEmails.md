# News-Triggered Email Templates

These templates are designed to leverage recent news mentions for personalized outreach. Each template focuses on a specific news category while maintaining a consistent structure.

## Email Structure

1. **Subject Line**: Reference both company name and news item
2. **Opening**: Acknowledge specific news with publication source
3. **Problem Statement**: Connect news to power challenges/opportunities
4. **Solution Overview**: Brief Mikano IPP value proposition
5. **Evidence**: Relevant case study (industry-matched)
6. **Call to Action**: Request brief conversation 
7. **Signature**: Professional signature with contact information

## Response Handling Guide

### If They Express Interest

Thank them promptly and offer specific time slots for a call or meeting.

```
Thank you for your response. I'd be happy to discuss this further at your convenience.

Would any of these times work for a 15-minute call:
• [Day], [Date] at [Time]
• [Day], [Date] at [Time]
• [Day], [Date] at [Time]

Alternatively, if you prefer a face-to-face meeting, I could visit your office next week.

I'll also send across our case study on [Similar Company in their industry] ahead of our conversation for your reference.

Looking forward to speaking with you.
```

### If They Redirect to Someone Else

Thank them and immediately reach out to the suggested contact.

```
Thank you for directing me to [New Contact]. I appreciate your assistance.

I'll reach out to them directly with the information about how Mikano's IPP solution might benefit [Company]'s [specific situation].

If there's anything else you think would be helpful for me to know before speaking with [New Contact], please let me know.
```

### If They Decline

Respect their decision but keep the door open for future contact.

```
I understand this may not be a priority at the moment. Thank you for considering it.

If it's helpful, I can send you a brief case study about how we've helped similar companies in your industry with power solutions that required no capital investment.

Please feel free to reach out if your situation changes or if you have any questions about optimizing your power infrastructure in the future.
```

## Customization Guidelines

For each template:

1. **Research Thoroughly**
   - Read the full news article, not just the headline
   - Visit company website for accurate information
   - Check LinkedIn for contact person's background

2. **Personalize Specifically**
   - Reference exact expansion location/amount
   - Mention precise funding amount/investor if public
   - Cite specific power challenges mentioned in news

3. **Industry-Match Examples**
   - Always use case studies from same industry when possible
   - Match company size and region when relevant
   - Use similar expansion/challenge scenarios
