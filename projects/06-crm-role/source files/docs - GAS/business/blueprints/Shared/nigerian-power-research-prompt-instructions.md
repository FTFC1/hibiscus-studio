# Nigerian Power Champion Research Framework - Prompt Engineering Instructions

## **Context for JSON Assistant**

You are creating research conversation frameworks for engaging Nigerian industrial power champions (middle management with operational power responsibilities). This is for high-value B2B gas sales targeting £2-5M annual contracts.

## **Critical Nigerian Market Context**

**Cultural Communication Requirements:**
- Indirect communication style (face-saving important)
- Relationship-building before business discussion (3-5 touchpoints)
- Hierarchy respect (titles, age/experience deference)
- Technical credibility must be established early
- Use "challenges/opportunities" not "problems"
- Personal relationships trump transactional efficiency

**Power Market Realities:**
- Grid reliability: 40-70% uptime (varies by location)
- Generator dependency: 85% of businesses >50 employees
- Diesel costs: ₦180-250 per kWh vs grid ₦60-120 per kWh
- Voltage fluctuations damage equipment (180V-250V swings)
- Foreign exchange impact: 40-60% of energy costs USD-denominated

**Industry-Specific Pain Points:**
- Manufacturing: Production shutdowns cost ₦2-10M per hour
- Banking: 99.9% uptime required for regulatory compliance
- Healthcare: Patient safety trumps cost considerations
- Telecoms: Network downtime costs ₦50M+ per hour for major operators

## **Target Power Champions by Industry**

**Manufacturing:** Plant Manager, Production Manager, Maintenance Manager
**Banking:** Facilities Manager, IT Infrastructure Manager, Operations Manager  
**Healthcare:** Facilities Manager, Biomedical Engineer, Operations Manager
**Telecoms:** Network Operations Manager, Infrastructure Manager, Site Acquisition Manager

## **Required JSON Framework Structure**

Create conversation frameworks with these sections:

1. **Relationship Building Phase** (Touchpoints 1-2)
   - Industry credibility establishment
   - Technical competence demonstration
   - Mutual connection leveraging

2. **Industry Discussion Phase** (Touchpoints 3-4)
   - General industry challenges (not company-specific)
   - Operational efficiency opportunities
   - Market trends and regulatory changes

3. **Company-Specific Discovery Phase** (Touchpoints 5+)
   - Current power infrastructure assessment
   - Operational impact quantification
   - Decision-making process mapping
   - Future planning and budget cycles

## **Question Design Principles**

**Cultural Sensitivity:**
- Start with acknowledging their expertise
- Reference industry best practices, not company problems
- Use consultative language ("In your experience..." "What trends are you seeing...")
- Avoid direct criticism of current solutions
- Frame as partnership opportunity, not vendor relationship

**Technical Credibility:**
- Demonstrate understanding of their specific operational challenges
- Reference relevant case studies from similar companies
- Use industry-specific terminology correctly
- Show awareness of regulatory/compliance requirements

**Business Intelligence Gathering:**
- Map decision-making hierarchy and influence patterns
- Understand budget cycles and approval processes
- Identify operational pain points with quantifiable impact
- Assess competitive landscape and current supplier relationships

For each industry, create:

   - Phase-by-phase question progression
   - Cultural sensitivity guidelines for each phase

2. **Industry-Specific Pain Point Mapping** with:

   - Influence mapping within organization
   - Technical credibility establishment methods

## **Success Metrics for Framework**

- Builds genuine relationship over 3-5 touchpoints
- Establishes technical credibility early
- Gathers actionable business intelligence
- Identifies decision-making process and timeline
- Positions for technical assessment opportunity
- Maintains cultural sensitivity throughout
