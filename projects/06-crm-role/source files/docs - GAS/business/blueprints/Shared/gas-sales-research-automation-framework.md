# Gas Sales Research & Targeting Automation Framework

## **System Overview**

**Objective:** Automate discovery, research, profiling, and targeting of 1000 Nigerian industrial companies for £1bn gas sales pipeline.

**Output:** Weekly batches of 20-50 companies with deep profiles ready for targeted outreach campaigns.

**Process:** 4-stage iterative research system with continuous learning and optimization.

## **Stage 1: Company Discovery Engine**

**Target:** Identify 1000 high-value Nigerian industrial companies (£500k+ annual power costs)

**Data Sources:**
- Nigerian Stock Exchange listings (manufacturing, telecoms, banking)
- Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) business registry
- Industry association member directories
- Government contractor databases
- LinkedIn company pages with employee counts
- News mentions of expansion/operations

**Filtering Criteria:**
- Annual revenue >₦5bn (indicates high power consumption)
- Employee count >200 (substantial operations)
- Industrial/commercial operations (not pure trading)
- Lagos/Abuja/Port Harcourt locations (grid access + backup needs)
- Recent expansion/investment news (growth = power needs)

**Output:** Master database with:
- Company name, industry, location, size
- Estimated annual power costs
- Growth indicators and expansion plans
- Competitive landscape position
- Initial priority scoring (1-10)

## **Stage 2: Power Champion Identification**

**Target:** Map 3-5 power champions per company (middle management with operational authority)

**Research Process:**
1. **LinkedIn Mapping**
   - Search company + power-related job titles
   - Map reporting structures and tenure
   - Identify recent hires/promotions (change = opportunity)

2. **Industry Network Analysis**
   - Professional associations (Nigerian Society of Engineers, etc.)
   - Conference speaker lists and attendees
   - Industry publication quotes/interviews

3. **News & Press Release Mining**
   - Operational announcements and project launches
   - Staff appointments and promotions
   - Technical challenges and solutions mentioned

**Target Roles by Industry:**
- Manufacturing: Plant Manager, Production Manager, Maintenance Manager, Engineering Manager
- Banking: Facilities Manager, IT Infrastructure Manager, Operations Manager, Risk Manager
- Healthcare: Facilities Manager, Biomedical Engineer, Operations Manager, Chief Medical Officer
- Telecoms: Network Operations Manager, Infrastructure Manager, Site Acquisition Manager

**Output:** Power champion profiles with:
- Name, title, tenure, reporting structure
- Professional background and expertise areas
- Recent projects/challenges mentioned publicly
- Communication preferences and channels
- Influence level and decision-making authority

## **Stage 3: Deep Company & Individual Profiling**

**8-Hour Equivalent Research Per Batch (20-50 companies)**

### **Company Intelligence Gathering**

**Operational Analysis:**
- Current power infrastructure (grid dependency, generator capacity)
- Recent operational challenges (outages, expansion, efficiency projects)
- Regulatory compliance requirements and deadlines
- Competitive positioning and market pressures
- Financial performance and investment capacity

**Decision-Making Mapping:**
- Budget cycles and approval processes
- Recent infrastructure investments and vendors
- Key stakeholders and influence patterns
- Procurement preferences (local vs international)
- Risk tolerance and innovation adoption

### **Individual Psychology & Persuasion Profiling**

**Professional Motivation Analysis:**
- Career trajectory and advancement goals
- Technical expertise and knowledge gaps
- Recent achievements and recognition
- Professional challenges and pain points
- Industry reputation and peer relationships

**Communication & Influence Patterns:**
- Preferred communication channels and timing
- Technical vs commercial language preferences
- Decision-making style (analytical, relationship-based, risk-averse)
- Influence tactics that resonate (data, peer validation, authority)
- Cultural and personal background factors

**Persuasion Strategy Development:**
- Primary motivators (efficiency, cost, safety, recognition)
- Objection patterns and resistance points
- Trust-building requirements and credibility factors
- Timing considerations and urgency drivers
- Relationship-building approach and touchpoint sequence

## **Stage 4: Campaign Preparation & Execution**

**Weekly Batch Output (20-50 companies):**

### **Email Campaign Assets**

- Technical credibility demonstrations and case studies
- Industry-specific pain point messaging

### **Company Profile Summaries**
- Power cost analysis and savings potential
- Operational challenges and gas solution fit
- Decision-making process and timeline

- Risk factors and mitigation strategies

### **Individual Engagement Strategies**

- Follow-up sequences and escalation paths
