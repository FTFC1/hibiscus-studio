## DECISIONS MADE

### 1. ABANDON Trigger Monitoring via RSS/LinkedIn (CRITICAL)

**Decision:** Stop building trigger monitoring systems (Google Alerts, Inoreader, LinkedIn automation)

**Why:**
- Nigerian triggers are private/unknowable via public data sources
- Examples: Pastor gets funding (no announcement), gen stolen (not newsworthy), inside deals (won't appear online)
- LinkedIn doesn't cover market (Chinese industrial parks, local buyers don't use Western platforms)
- Media is censored ("have to approve what they post") ‚Üí delays, bias, gaps

**Impact:**
- 8 implementation guides created (bookmarklet, alerts dashboard, etc.) are now DEPRIORITIZED
- Focus shifts from "find triggers yourself" to "build network that tells you about triggers"

**Alternative Approach:**
- Referral engine (people tell you about triggers)
- Authority/inbound (they find you when triggered)
- Physical presence (hear triggers in conversation)
- Strategic partnerships (piggyback on deal flow)

**Decision:** Don't focus on "Head of Operations at end customers" as primary target
