What Is This Program?

A cross-selling and upselling game that teaches your sales staff how to sell more — on their phones, during work. Staff play games, complete short lessons, and take quizzes. A manager dashboard tracks who's progressing and who needs a nudge.

What staff will do:

  • Play 3 sales games (Customer Says, The Approach, Build Your Basket)
  • Read short lessons (3-5 minutes each)
  • Pass quizzes (80% threshold, can retry)
  • Practice techniques on the shop floor

What this is NOT:

  • Not a classroom course. No sitting in a room for hours.
  • Not a textbook. Content is game-first, then lessons.
  • Not extra work. 15 minutes, 3 times a week. That's it.
App link: puma-training.vercel.app
Works on: Any phone with a browser (Chrome, Safari). No download needed.

Onboarding Steps (Week 1)

1 Collect Staff Information

Gather from each of the 7 pilot staff:

  • Full name
  • Email address (for account creation)
  • Phone number (for Telegram group)
  • Confirm they have a smartphone with browser access

Time: 10-15 minutes (can do via WhatsApp group message)

2 Schedule Physical Onboarding Session

Staff need to be physically together for the first session. This is where they:

  • Download Telegram (if not already installed)
  • Join the "PUMA Sales Mastery" Telegram group
  • Open the training app in their browser
  • Log in with their credentials
  • Try the first game together (builds excitement)
This must be in-person. Sending a link by WhatsApp alone won't work. Staff need to see each other using it, ask questions live, and feel that this is real — not optional.

Best time: Before store opens or during a quiet period. 30-45 minutes.

Time: 30-45 minutes (coordinate with store manager)

3 Send Staff Details to FORJE

Send the collected names + emails to FORJE (Nicholas) via WhatsApp or email. FORJE will:

  • Create accounts for each staff member
  • Send login credentials back to you
  • Set up the Telegram group

Time: 5 minutes (one message)

4 Distribute Credentials

At the physical onboarding session, give each staff member:

  • The app link: puma-training.vercel.app
  • Their login email and password
  • The Telegram group invite link

Walk through the first login together. Make sure everyone can see their home screen before leaving.

Time: 15 minutes (part of the physical session)

5 Confirm Enrollment with Manager

After the onboarding session, confirm with the store manager:

  • All 7 staff are logged in (check dashboard shows 7 users)
  • All 7 are in the Telegram group
  • Manager knows to check dashboard Mon/Wed/Fri
  • Manager has the Manager Guide (separate document)

Time: 5 minutes (quick confirmation)

Week 1 Checklist

The dashboard gives you data you can use in performance reviews:

Recommendation: After the 6-week trial, training completion status can become part of the quarterly review criteria. Staff who complete all modules demonstrate initiative and willingness to develop skills.

Adding New Staff (After Pilot)

When a new hire joins after the pilot is running:

  1. Collect name + email + phone number
  2. Send to FORJE — account created within 24 hours
  3. Existing staff member walks them through first login (peer onboarding)
  4. They join the same Telegram group
  5. They start from Mission 1 (everyone goes through the same content)
No extra cost. The platform fee is flat — whether 7 or 50 staff use it, the cost doesn't change. Adding new hires is free.
