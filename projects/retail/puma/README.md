### Module 1: Customer Engagement Foundations (Weeks 1-2)
**Missions 1-6:**
- Timing & approach (5-30-60 Rule)
- Warm openings (kill "Can I help you?")
- Active listening (70/30 Rule, 4 Questions Framework)
- Objection handling (hesitation, price, exit protocol)

### Module 2: Cross-Selling & Upselling (Weeks 3-4)
**Missions 7-12:**
- Automatic add-ons
- Outfit Complete framework
- Bundle building
- Cross-sell timing
- "I only came for X" objections
- Upselling without pushiness

**Missions 13-18:**
- PUMA heritage & positioning
- Product categories & key features
- Quality indicators
- Competitor comparisons
- Handling brand objections
- Building expert credibility

**Missions 19-24:**
- Top 20 SKUs (key products)
- Common objections per category
- Seasonal selling strategies
- Customer segmentation
- Store-specific protocols (PRL Lekki)
- Case studies & wins
