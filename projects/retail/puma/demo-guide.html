01
What This Is
A mobile-first training app where sales staff learn customer engagement techniques through bite-sized lessons, practice checklists, and interactive games — then track their progress on a leaderboard.
Not a PDF. Not slides. An actual app.
Staff log in on their phones, swipe through lessons during quiet moments, practice techniques on the floor, then play games to reinforce what they learned. Manager sees who's ahead, who needs help.

03
User Flows
Staff Experience
1
Home — See your current mission, progress bar, team activity. Tap "Start Session" to begin.
Missions unlock sequentially. Complete one to unlock the next.
2
Lessons — Swipe through 5 slides: problem → rule → toolkit → comparison → practice tasks.
Each lesson is 5-8 minutes. Tap "Complete Mission" when done.
3
Practice — Daily checklist tied to what you learned. Check off tasks during your shift.
Includes end-of-day mood check (after 5pm).
4
Games — "Spot the Difference" (pick the better sales approach) and "Role Play" (handle customer scenarios).
Scores save to your profile and leaderboard.
5
Activity — Leaderboard + team buzz. See who's ahead, compete for top spot.
6
Profile — Stats, badges, certification progress, logout.
Manager Experience
1
Dashboard — Alerts (who's behind) → Wins (who's performing) → Full staff list.
Admin Dashboard Principle: What's wrong? What's good? What needs attention?
2
Role Switcher — On Profile page, managers can toggle between Manager and Staff views.
Same app, two experiences.

05
Why This Beats the Alternatives
vs. PDF Training Manuals
Nobody reads them. No tracking. No accountability. No feedback loop. You print them, hand them out, hope for the best.
vs. WhatsApp Groups
Messages get buried. No structure. Can't track who completed what. No scoring. No games. No progression.
vs. Classroom Training
Expensive. Takes staff off the floor. One session, then forgotten. This app reinforces daily — on the floor, during quiet moments.
vs. Generic LMS (Udemy, etc.)
Not built for retail. Not mobile-first. Not tied to daily practice. Not gamified for sales staff. This is purpose-built.
