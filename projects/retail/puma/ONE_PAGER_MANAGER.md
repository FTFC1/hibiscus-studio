## Practical Task Verification (Your Key Responsibility)

**Each mission has a practical task** (e.g., "Use Opening #3 with 2 customers today").

**How to verify:**

**Option 1: Direct observation** (best for Missions 1-3)
- Watch staff interact with customer (5 mins)
- Fill out simple checklist on phone (Did they make eye contact in 5 seconds? ‚úÖ)
- Upload photo of checklist to dashboard

**Option 2: Self-report + spot check** (Missions 4-6)
- Staff describes what they did (100 words in Telegram)
- Peer witness confirms ("I saw Tunde do this")
- You spot-check 1-2 randomly per week

**Option 3: Video role-play** (objection handling missions)
- Staff records 60-second role-play with co-worker
- Uploads to dashboard
- You approve/reject

**Time commitment:** 30-45 mins/week total for 7 staff
