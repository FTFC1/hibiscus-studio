The Problem

Customer says 'I need running shoes.' You rattle off every feature. Their eyes glaze over. They leave. You feature-dumped instead of solving their problem.

The 70/30 Rule

70% Customer talks
30% You talk

If you're doing most of the talking, you're doing it wrong.

Ask These 4 Questions

1. What are you buying for?
2. What's not working now?
3. What matters most?
4. Buying today or browsing?

Only show products after all 4.

Spot the Difference

Don't

"You show 5 shoes and list features. They leave."

Do

"You ask 4 questions, show 2 options that match. They buy."

Try This Today

Pick 3 customers — use all 4 questions
Count: are they talking more than you?
Write down what you learned at end of shift
