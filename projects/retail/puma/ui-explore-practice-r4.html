Kill "Can I Help You?"
Replace the question that kills conversations before they start.
Tap to review
Why It Fails
Closed question. Customer says "just looking" reflexively. Conversation dead.
Instead: Comment on what they're looking at. Be a person first, salesperson second.
"Those just came in yesterday" "That's our best seller this month"
Customer picks up a shoe, checks the price, puts it down. Don't ask if they need help — say "That one runs really well for the price. What are you using it for?"
