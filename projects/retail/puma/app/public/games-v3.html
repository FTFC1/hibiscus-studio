V3 Design Decisions

  • Glass morphism: Cards use gradient bg + backdrop-blur + subtle top highlight (SN #47 Opacity & Blur, #41 Dark UI).
  • Gradient buttons: Lime gradient instead of flat — adds depth without weight (SN #38 Gradients).
  • Glow accents: Icon boxes emit subtle lime glow — creates focal points in dark UI (SN #46 Depth & Shadow).
  • Numbered progress: Circled numbers with connecting lines — more game-like than dots (SN #30 Affordance).
  • Score history: "Best: 80%" pill on game cards — creates replayability incentive (UXD Goal Gradient Effect).
  • Consistent gradients: All surfaces use 135deg gradient angle — unified direction of light (SN #41 Dark UI).
