V1 Design Decisions

  • Elevation: Cards lifted with box-shadow (Shift Nudge #46 — depth + shadow). Creates tactile feel.
  • Spacing: 20px padding inside cards, 16px gap between (SN #22 Box Model, #28 Density). Generous breathing room.
  • Touch targets: 44px play button, 52px icon — exceeds Apple's 44pt minimum (SN #78 iOS Design).
  • Typography: 16px titles (up from 14), clear size steps: 16/13/11/10 (SN #11 Font Size, #13 Hierarchy).
  • Section labels: "Available Now" / "Coming Soon" groups cards with purpose (SN #69 Lists & Cards).
  • Corner radius: 20px cards, 16px icons, 14px choices — consistent nested radius (SN #44 Corner Radius).
