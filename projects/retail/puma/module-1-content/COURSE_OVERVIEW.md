# Cross-Sell & Upsell Mastery
## Training Course Overview

**Target Audience:** Retail sales staff (PUMA PRL Lekki pilot)
**Duration:** 5-6 weeks (10 minutes/day)
**Total Missions:** 24
**Delivery:** Mobile-first, bite-sized learning

---

## Course Goal

Increase basket size and repeat purchases by teaching systematic cross-sell and upsell techniques that feel natural, not pushy.

**Success Metrics:**
- Average basket size increases by 20-30%
- Add-on attachment rate reaches 70%+
- Customer satisfaction remains high (no pushy selling)
- Staff confidence in suggesting products increases

---

## Course Structure

### Module 1: Foundation ‚Äî The 5 Moments
**Duration:** 1.5 weeks (6 missions)
**Why:** Can't cross-sell if you don't know how to engage customers properly

| Mission | Topic | Key Outcome |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | The 5-30-60 Rule | Every customer acknowledged within 5 sec, approached within 60 sec |
| 2 | Kill "Can I Help You?" | Use warm openings that invite conversation |
| 3 | The 70/30 Rule | Customer talks 70%, you talk 30% ‚Äî read needs before recommending |
| 4 | "I'll Think About It" | Handle delay objections, capture contact info |
| 5 | "It's Too Expensive" | Build price confidence, justify value |
| 6 | Exit Protocol | Capture details from non-buyers, set up follow-up |

**By end of Module 1:** Staff can confidently engage customers, identify needs, and handle basic objections.

---

### Module 2: Cross-Sell Systems ‚Äî THE MONEY MODULE
**Duration:** 2 weeks (8 missions)
**Why:** This is where basket size grows

| Mission | Topic | Key Outcome |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| 7 | The Automatic Add-On | Default pairings for every product (shoes + socks, etc.) |
| 8 | The Outfit Complete | Full-look selling (turn 1 item into 3-4) |
| 9 | Care = Cash | Maintenance products as natural upsells |
| 10 | The Bundle Builder | Package deals that feel like value |
| 11 | The Future Need | Set up replenishment cycle |
| 12 | The Size-Up | Volume increase (2-pack vs 1, larger size) |
| 13 | Reading Buy Signals | When to suggest, when to hold back |
| 14 | The Second Look | Comeback upsells ("try one more thing?") |

**By end of Module 2:** Every transaction includes at least one suggested add-on. Basket size increases measurably.

---

### Module 3: Upsell Psychology
**Duration:** 1.5 weeks (6 missions)
**Why:** Teaching WHEN and HOW to suggest premium alternatives

| Mission | Topic | Key Outcome |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| 15 | The Trade-Up | When to suggest premium vs. sticking with original |
| 16 | The Comparison Frame | Good/Better/Best positioning |
| 17 | The Try-Before-Decide | Get them to experience premium first |
| 18 | The Celebration Upsell | Special occasions justify premium |
| 19 | The Quality Story | Justifying premium pricing with storytelling |
| 20 | The No-Regret Close | Reduce buyer's remorse on big purchases |

**By end of Module 3:** Staff confidently suggest premium alternatives when appropriate, without being pushy.
