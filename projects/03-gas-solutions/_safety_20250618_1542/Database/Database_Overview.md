# Nigerian Companies with High Power Requirements

This directory contains data about Nigerian companies with high power requirements that are potential candidates for Independent Power Plant (IPP) conversions.

## Data Files

### CSV and Excel Files
- `scraped_companies_*.csv` - CSV export of companies identified through web scraping
- `scraped_companies_*.xlsx` - Excel version of the same data
- `power_companies_*.csv` - CSV export of companies identified through crawling
- `power_companies_*.xlsx` - Excel version of the same data
- `sector_breakdown.xlsx` - Analysis of companies by industry sector
- `regional_breakdown.xlsx` - Analysis of companies by geographic region
- `energy_companies_tracker.xlsx` - Tracking file for outreach to identified companies

### Markdown Files
- `dangote_profile.md` - Sample detailed profile of Dangote Group
- `top_leads.md` - Summary of the highest-priority leads
- `news_scraping_companies_summary.md` - Summary of companies from news scraping
- `company_leads_summary.md` - Overall summary of all identified leads
- `execution_report.md` - Report on the execution of scraping activities

### HTML Files
- `dangote_profile.html` - HTML version of Dangote Group profile
- `top_leads.html` - HTML version of top leads for web viewing

## Categorized Leads

The following directories contain leads organized by different criteria:

- `By_Industry/` - Companies organized by industry sector
- `By_Priority/` - Companies organized by priority level
- `By_Region/` - Companies organized by geographic region

## Data Structure

Each company record typically includes:

- **Company Name**: The name of the identified company
- **Industry**: The industry or sector the company operates in
- **Power Mentions**: Number of power-related keywords found in association with the company
- **Location**: Primary location of operations in Nigeria
- **News Summary**: Brief summary of the news article where the company was mentioned
- **News URL**: Source URL for the company information
- **Date Found**: When the company was identified
- **Power Related Text**: Specific text snippets related to power needs

## Usage

This data is intended for internal use by the sales team to identify and prioritize outreach to companies that would benefit from IPP solutions.
