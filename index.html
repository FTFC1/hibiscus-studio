Common Questions

What is the capacity? +
We can seat up to 25 guests for intimate events. For larger groups (up to 40), please enquire and we will confirm the setup that works best.
Can I bring my own food and drinks? +
Yes. Our kitchen includes a fridge, 5-hob stove, microwave, and double oven. Outside catering is welcome. We do not provide cooking utensils, and the space must be left as you found it.
Is parking available? +
Paid parking is available on nearby streets. Free parking after 6:30 PM and all day on Sundays. There is a 30-minute loading zone at the entrance for drop-offs.
Can I view the studio before booking? +
Of course. We offer viewing slots so you can see the space in person before committing. Send us a message to arrange a time. Viewings are free and usually take about 15 minutes.
Can I book extra time beyond the package? +
Yes, additional hours can be arranged (subject to availability) and are charged at £100 per hour. These are invoiced separately once confirmed.
Can I request changes to my booking? +
Changes must be requested at least 7 days in advance, subject to availability. Please contact us as early as possible.
What happens if something gets damaged? +
Your £200 damages deposit covers any issues. If the space passes inspection, the full deposit is returned within 1–3 working days. If repairs or extra cleaning are needed, costs are deducted from the deposit.
Do you provide cooking equipment or utensils? +
The kitchen appliances (stove, oven, fridge, microwave) are included. However, we do not provide cooking utensils, pots, pans, or ingredients. Please bring what you need for your event.
