Frequently Asked Questions

What's the capacity? +

40 people seated. 70 standing.

How to secure my booking +

Choose your booking type. Reserve your date on our calendar. We will invoice you within 48 hours. Once your 50% deposit is received your booking is confirmed. If we do not receive your deposit within 48 hours we will cancel your booking.

Can I bring my own food & drinks? +

Absolutely. Our kitchenette is yours to use and includes a fridge, 5-hob stove, microwave, and double oven. Outside catering is welcome. Please note: we do not provide any cooking utensils or equipment, and the space must be left as you found it.

What's the cancellation policy? +

• More than 30 days before: Full refund of the deposit, or reschedule without fees (subject to availability). • 15–30 days before: 50% of the deposit refunded, or one free reschedule (must be confirmed within 14 days of the original booking). • 7–14 days before: The deposit is non-refundable. Rescheduling may be allowed for a £25 admin fee (subject to availability). • Less than 7 days before: The deposit is forfeited, and no rescheduling is permitted. • No-show or same-day cancellations: The full booking amount will be charged as a no-show fee.

Is parking available? +

Parking is the client's responsibility. Paid parking is available on nearby streets. Parking is free after 6:30 PM and all day on Sundays. There's also a 30-minute loading zone at the entrance for drop-offs and collections.

What about setup and pack down time? +

For Event Hire, a complimentary hour is included at the start or end of your booking (subject to availability) — this applies only to Half Day and Full Day packages. Please ensure you allow enough time for setup and pack down when reserving your slot.

Your hire includes: +

Your hire includes:
• 40 limewash Chiavari chairs
• 10 trestle tables (each 6ft x 2ft)

Can I bring a DJ or play music? +

Yes, DJs are welcome! Please note we do not supply speakers or a PA system — these must be brought with you. Please also ensure noise levels are respectful; excessively loud music or any noise complaints will result in the loss of your damages deposit.
